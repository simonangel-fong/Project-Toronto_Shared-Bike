User Behavior Insight

Seasonal Patterns

Both annual and casual users ride more frequently during the warmer season.

Annual users maintain steady usage year-round, taking shorter trips that reflect routine, purpose-driven travel such as commuting.

Casual users ride less during winter but take longer trips in summer, indicating usage primarily for recreation or leisure.

Hourly Patterns

Both annual and casual users are most active during rush-hour periods.

Annual users maintain short, consistent trip durations throughout the day, reflecting planned, purpose-driven usage, such as commuting.

Casual users generally take longer trips, suggesting occasional, convenience-focused or leisure use. However, they also make shorter trips during the morning peak.
