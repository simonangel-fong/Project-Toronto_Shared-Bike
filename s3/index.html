User Behavior Insight

Distinct Seasonal and User Patterns

Bike usage peaks in the warmer months, especially among casual members, while annual members maintain steadier usage throughout the year, reflecting commuting versus recreational use.

Clear Hourly Usage Trends

Annual members primarily use bikes during morning and evening commute hours, whereas casual members favor afternoon and evening rides, highlighting differing travel purposes.

Longer Rides for Casual Members

Average trip durations are significantly higher for casual members compared to annual members, emphasizing leisure and tourism-driven usage among casual riders and more practical, shorter trips among annual users.

Steady Year-over-Year Growth

Toronto’s bike share program has seen a consistent increase in total trips from 2019 to 2023, indicating rising popularity and integration into the city’s transportation network.
